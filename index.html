The future of travel

Are you tired of your friends failing to pay you back for that uber ride?

Trip Split allows you to effortlessly split the cost of any ride. No more excuses from your friends.

Our easy to use app for both Android and IOS is compatible with every major ride-hailing service. Request payment with just a touch of a button.

No cash? No problem

Trip split accepts all debit/credit cards and has built in Android Pay and Apple Pay integration for seamless transactions.

Designed by ride-hailing enthusiasts

Here at Trip Split we've tried almost ever ride-hailing service and they all suffer from the same problem. One of us has to pay, and the other has to some how remember to make good on the promise of paying their share back (which almost never happens). That is why we developed Trip Split.
